Our Return Policy

‚úÖ Simple 30-Day Returns

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not completely satisfied, you can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Items must be:

  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • In original, unused condition
  • In original packaging with all tags attached
  • Accompanied by proof of purchase

How to Return an Item

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and go to "My Orders". Select the order you want to return and click "Return Items".

2

Print Label

Print the prepaid return shipping label provided. No need to pay for return shipping!

3

Pack Items

Pack items securely in original packaging (or similar). Attach the return label to the outside of the box.

4

Ship It Back

Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location. You'll receive a tracking number via email.

5

Get Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Information

üí∞ Refund Processing

Timeline: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the refund to appear in your account.

Refund Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Store Credit: Instant (upon return processing)

Note: Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error (defective item, wrong item shipped, etc.).
